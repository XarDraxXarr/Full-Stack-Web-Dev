Anime Heroes & Their Powers

Goku
The Saiyan warrior who loves fighting strong opponents, eats like a monster, and protects Earth with his signature Kamehameha wave.
Ash Ketchum
The eternal 10-year-old Pokémon Trainer who dreams of being a Pokémon Master. Pikachu's best friend!
Vegeta
The proud Saiyan Prince, Goku's rival, and later ally. Known for his arrogance, Galick Gun, and endless training sessions.
